Cancer Organizations in Northern Virginia

A Comprehensive Resource for SYNERGY HomeCare's Cancer Care Initiative

About This Resource

This comprehensive dashboard provides information about cancer-focused organizations, advocacy groups, and support groups in the Northern Virginia area. It is designed to support SYNERGY HomeCare's cancer care initiative, helping to identify potential partners and resources for individuals in need of care services during their cancer journey.

SYNERGY HomeCare Services

Our caregivers provide specialized support for individuals with cancer, including assistance with daily activities, transportation to medical appointments, medication reminders, meal preparation, and compassionate companionship throughout treatment and recovery.

Address:

13800 Coppermine Road, Suite 116
Herndon, VA 20171

Description:

Cancer Can Rock is a non-profit organization founded by Jim Ebert, a multi-platinum record producer and cancer survivor. Unlike most cancer-based charities, Cancer Can Rock doesn't seek a cure, rather it strives to serve a singular community: Musicians facing aggressive cancer.

Services:

  • Professional recording studio experience for musicians with cancer
  • Audio and video production
  • Support for musicians fighting aggressive cancer
  • Preservation of musical legacy
